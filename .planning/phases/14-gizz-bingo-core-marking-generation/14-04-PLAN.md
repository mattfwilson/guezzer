<objective>
Implement `deal(seed, vibe, ctx, dexSnapshot, corpusVersion, cfg) → BingoCard`: the pure seeded card
generator (D-21). All randomness is drawn from the string-seeded PRNG stream (`${seed} ${vibe}
${corpusVersion}` → xmur3 → mulberry32) — never `Math.random`/`Date.now`. It selects squares from the
v1 auto-mark catalog (D-24) weighted by the per-vibe mix (D-04/D-14/D-18), resolves them into
self-contained frozen defs, places the free cell at `freeIndex` (D-06), and returns a schema-validated
16-square card. Same inputs → byte-identical card; a complete card is guaranteed (never blank).

Purpose: BINGO-01's substrate and the calibration input — the gate (Plan 05) deals N seeded cards per
vibe and replays them. Freezing resolved defs (not seed-regenerated at read time) is what lets Phase-15
persist a locked card safely.
Output: generate.ts + generate.test.ts (reproducibility + never-blank + catalog coverage).
</objective>
