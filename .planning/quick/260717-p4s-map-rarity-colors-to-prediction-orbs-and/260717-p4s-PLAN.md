<objective>
Map the five rarity-tier colors (plus the neutral "debut" state) onto the Show Mode
prediction orbs — the live prediction fan, the current-song center orb, and the
chronological comet trail / full-setlist rings — so a user reads a prediction's
rarity at a glance, and route EVERY rarity surface in the app through one reusable
styling primitive so future tier-color changes propagate from a single place.

Today the prediction orbs, center node, and comet trail are colored by TUNING
FAMILY (`tuningColor`). The tier chips (GizzDex `SongRow`/`DexHeader`, recap,
compare, retro-setlist) and the share card already read the consolidated
`config.dex.tierColors` map (from quick task 260717-onl) but each reaches into it
directly. This task (a) creates the single `rarityStyle` UI primitive, (b) switches
the Show Mode orb/trail surfaces from tuning to rarity color, and (c) refactors the
existing direct consumers to the primitive — with `config.dex.tierColors` remaining
the one source of truth and no new hardcoded hexes.

Purpose: a consistent, learnable rarity color language across the whole app, wired
so it can never drift again.
Output: `rarityStyle.ts` primitive + all rarity surfaces (chips, share card, orbs,
trail) consuming it.
</objective>
