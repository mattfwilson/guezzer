    2. **Head-to-head (PROG-06 live):** On device B, tap device A's row → confirm the full-screen head-to-head opens, "You vs {A}" columns are populated, the per-album/per-rarity breakdown expands, and A's rarest-catches showcase renders. Confirm the numbers match what A's own GizzDex shows.
    3. **Reconnect flush (D-17):** On device A, go offline (airplane mode / devtools offline). Confirm the Friends list shows dimmed cached rows + `Offline · as of {time}`, and the "You" row stays live. Log 1–2 catches while offline. Re-enable connectivity. Confirm: the offline marker clears, rows un-dim, device A's own row flushes its fresh summary, and device B sees A's new numbers within a few seconds.
    4. **Never-blank venue view (D-18):** Confirm that at no point during the offline window is the Friends area blank or spinning — cached rows + the live "You" row are always present.
