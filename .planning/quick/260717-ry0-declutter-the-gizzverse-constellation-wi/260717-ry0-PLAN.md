<objective>
Declutter the GizzVerse (Explore) constellation. At the current global `count>=2`
threshold 1041 edges draw — a hairball. Replace the "global min edge count" slider
with degree-aware top-K-per-node sparsification: keep each song's K highest-count OUT
edges (default K=2 gives ~332 edges, −68%). Re-purpose the existing EXPL-04 slider to
"top N per song" (default 2, range 1–5). On focus, reveal the focused node's FULL real
neighborhood regardless of the top-K gate. Bow reciprocal edge pairs apart with native
`linkCurvature`.

Purpose: legible dense-cluster rendering while preserving the single-pipeline +
settle-and-freeze ethos (EXPL-06) — every change is a pure render-pass filter/prop, no
graphData rebuild, no d3 reheat, frozen fx/fy survive.

Output: a pure core `topKEdgesPerNode` (fixture-tested), a renamed single-source config
constant set, and the canvas/UI wiring that consumes them.
</objective>
