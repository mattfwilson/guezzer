<objective>
Slice 2 — tap a node to see where the band goes next. A single tap focuses a node (gold ring, neighborhood highlight, everything else dimmed to 12%, forced labels), pans it into the upper 60% of the viewport, and opens a 40%-peek bottom sheet of ranked outgoing next-song bars with honest counts and dates. Tapping a bar chain-hops to that song — the core "walk the setlist paths" exploration loop.

Output: Focus+context highlighting on canvas + the ranked-bars sheet with chain-hop navigation.
</objective>
