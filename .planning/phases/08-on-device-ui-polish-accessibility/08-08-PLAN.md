<objective>
Close the POLISH-01 regression found on real iPhone hardware: many smaller prediction orbs OVERFLOW their song-name labels outside the circular fill.

Root cause (08-HUMAN-UAT.md Gaps + .planning/debug/orb-label-overflow-small-orbs.md): `fitOrbLabel` models the orb as a RECTANGLE. It grants the full diameter as usable text chord for every wrapped line (USABLE_WIDTH_FACTOR = 1) and never checks total text height against the circle's vertical extent. On small orbs (real min ORB_MIN_DIAMETER = 56px, passed raw) long names wrap to 3–4 near-full-diameter lines that satisfy the rectangular char budget (`ellipsized: false`) but spill past the circular chord and the vertical fill. PredictionOrb also passes the raw diameter (never subtracting its `px-1` face padding, unlike CenterNode) and renders a fixed 14px percent line below the name that the fitter reserves no room for. The catalog test only asserted `.ellipsized === false` at 3 fixed sizes, so it never validated circular fit — the coverage gap that let this ship.

The diagnosed bug is OVERFLOW, not truncation. Text spilling OUTSIDE the circle is the failure; graceful ellipsis (which stays INSIDE the circle) is the CORRECT fallback. This plan makes the hard, ungameable bar "no name overflows at any real orb diameter [56..112]" — a bar the honest circular geometry CAN satisfy — and asserts the stronger "no ellipsis at all" only where the honest geometry actually reaches it (the full catalog is clean at d ≥ 88; below that, small orbs may legitimately truncate a handful of the longest titles inside the circle).

Purpose: Make the fit heuristic geometrically honest (circle, not rectangle), guard it with a geometric no-overflow regression test that fails-before / passes-after, pin honesty floors so the test can't be bought with optimistic constants, and fix the on-device harness's rectangular blind spot so POLISH-01 can be re-verified truthfully on the owner's phone.

Output: Circle-aware `fitOrbLabel` + exported geometric predicate (per-line chord + percent-line chord + total height), retuned/added config constants with pinned honesty floors, padding-subtracting callers, a geometric catalog test swept over [56..112], and a circular-boundary OrbFitHarness. Core/UI separation preserved (all changes live in packages/app; no core, schema, or artifact edits). No scattered magic numbers — every geometric constant lives in config.ts.
</objective>
