<objective>
Namespace the six untagged Dexie tables to a user id without regressing the strictly-additive migration discipline, and make the pre-existing single-user dex claimable by the first signer exactly once (AUTH-05 / D-08/D-09/D-11). This is the local-data-isolation half of AUTH-05: the schema change (`version(7)`), the app-side one-time claim, and the export/import scoping. It has zero UI or auth-call dependency, so it runs in Wave 1 parallel to Plan 01. The read-scoping half (useDexStats) lands in Plan 06 where the current userId is available at the gate.

Purpose: A borrowed/shared phone must never cross-contaminate two friends' dexes. The claim preserves the owner's real v1 catch history as their identity's dex; subsequent friends see their own (empty until Phase 19) dex. Export/import must not leak or merge across identities.
Output: `db.ts` `version(7)` + `userId` field + scoped `snapshot`/`importSnapshot`; `claimDex.ts`; `migrationV7.test.ts`.
</objective>
