<objective>
Build the sign-in surface (AUTH-01 / D-01/D-03/D-04/D-18): a name-picker over a bundled roster of large one-thumb tap targets, a password field, the `signInWithPassword` call against the pre-made account, a calm generic inline error, and the offline "connect once" branch. On a successful sign-in it writes the app-owned identity record (Plan 03) and runs the one-time Dexie claim (Plan 02) — the two side effects that turn "authenticated" into "this identity owns this device's dex". This is a leaf surface with no gate dependency, so it runs in Wave 2 parallel to the identity chrome (Plan 05); the gate (Plan 06) mounts it.

Purpose: This is the only way in (auth is a full gate, D-02). It must be drunk-thumb / in-the-dark usable, must never leak real emails from the public bundle (D-04), and must never enumerate users on a bad password (D-18).
Output: `roster.ts`, `SignInScreen.tsx`, `signIn.test.tsx`.
</objective>
