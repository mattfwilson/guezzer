<objective>
On the LiveGizz (Show) page, BEFORE any next song is selected, crossfade the blurred
ambient background between RANDOM bundled album covers every 5 seconds. The moment the
user selects a next song (tap-orb or search-select — both funnel through
`session.currentSongId`), the existing behavior takes over: the background locks to that
song's album cover and cycling stops. Under `prefers-reduced-motion`, or when fewer than
2 covers are bundled, the background stays static on a single random cover (today's
behavior).

Purpose: A living, ambient pre-show backdrop that reads as texture and anticipation while
the user waits for the opener, without touching the already-correct selected-song path.

Output: `PRESHOW_CYCLE_MS` config constant + a gated cycling `ambientCover` in ShowView.
Purely app-layer decoration — no core changes, no ShowBackground changes.
</objective>
