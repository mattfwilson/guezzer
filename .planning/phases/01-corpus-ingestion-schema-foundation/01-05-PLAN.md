|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-13 | Tampering | albums.json ingestion | mitigate | artist_id filter + assert; only `song_url` slug and album name are read; `album_notes` (raw HTML) never parsed or emitted into the tags file |
| T-01-14 | Tampering/Integrity | hand-edited tuning-tags.json | mitigate | tuningTagsFileSchema strict-validates on every load (closed family enum, no duplicate songIds) — a typo'd hand-edit fails loudly instead of silently feeding Phase 2 garbage |
