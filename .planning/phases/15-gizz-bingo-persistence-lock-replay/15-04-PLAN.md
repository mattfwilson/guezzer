<objective>
Deliver BINGO-06 catch-up: a "Catch me up" surface on the active show that lets a
late-joining user bulk-add the songs the tracker missed (a pre-checked confirm-list
sourced from the live `latest` feed, committed via the shipped `adoptSuggestion`
path) plus manually search-and-log any square the feed missed. Catch-up NEVER touches
a square directly — it grows the trail, and `deriveMarks` re-lights squares as a pure
consequence (D-03/D-04/D-23), keeping the honest hit/miss denominator.

venue feed (glance-and-correct, not silent auto-adopt).

re-lights exactly the qualifying squares.
</objective>
