# Quick Task 260717-02n: LiveGizz ambient cover background

Implements todo `2026-07-17-blur-a-random-album-cover-as-the-show-screen-background`.
Replace the LiveGizz (Show) page's flat background with a randomized album cover,
heavily blurred and dark-dimmed, so the page feels visual while body content
(orbit, buttons, text) stays legible. Fully offline (covers are bundled/precached).

## Design decisions (owner idea; Claude's discretion on open questions)

- **Which cover:** fully random from the 29 bundled cover thumbs (not biased to
  caught) — simplest for v1.
- **Stability:** pick ONCE when `ShowView` mounts (`useState` initializer in the
  always-mounted parent), so it stays fixed across the pre-show → active → end
  transitions and never flickers mid-show. (Re-pick-per-night is a possible later
  enhancement — noted in SUMMARY.)
- **Offline:** the 160×160 WebP thumbs are already bundled via `import.meta.glob`
  in `covers.ts` and precached by the SW; heavy blur hides their low resolution.
- **Scope:** applies to the three in-page states (PreShowLauncher, matrix-fail,
  orbit). RecapView is EXCLUDED (its own post-show payoff screen).
