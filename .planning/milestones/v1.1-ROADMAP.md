## Milestone Summary

**Decimal Phases:** none.

**Key Decisions:**

- A11Y: one shared portal-to-body `<Sheet>` primitive + a module-level LIFO dialog stack (one shared keydown listener) drives Escape/focus-trap/focus-restore across all 7 surfaces; z-index centralized in `config.ui.z` (never Tailwind `z-[…]`) so there is one source of truth.
- POLISH-01: orb-label fit is circle-aware (per-line chord + height budget incl. the percent line), guarded by a geometric sweep over the real [56..112]px diameter range — rectangular fit over-granted width and ignored height, spilling small orbs on iPhone.
- DATA-06: fail-loud vs carry-tolerant split — prose fields (`shownotes`, footnotes) record to `NormalizeStats` and never throw; structural fields hard-fail. Position-1-wins denormalization; byte-for-byte verbatim carry; `schemaVersion` stays literal 1 (additive). `shownotes` lives in `corpus.json` and never reaches the bundled archive (archive.json byte-identical inside its 250 KB budget).
- PWA-05: extracted pure `isTypedNameMine` helper so the name-match path reaches merge, proven with a real-Dexie union-merge that never drops local data.
- VALID-01 (D-03): 9 *Infest the Rats' Nest* tracks re-tagged standard→cs-standard (first non-empty cs-standard family); backtest top-k zero regression (tuning is a weak signal, ablation Δ≈0).
- VALID-02: real-device iPhone rehearsal on the Vite production build; 2 D-09 loop-breaking blockers found+fixed inline (SuggestionStrip slot sizing/overflow; FAB lifted above the reserved strip); owner-directed scope expansion added a per-show recap Share card (core `buildRecapShareStats` off `deriveRecap`) replacing the mismatched lifetime-GizzDex card.

**Issues Resolved:**

- v1.0 audit WR-01 (`shownotes` dropped at normalization) — closed by DATA-06.
- POLISH-01 small-orb overflow regression found on-device — closed by the 08-08 circle-aware gap-closure plan.
- Two D-09 show-loop blockers (SuggestionStrip sizing; FAB over the reserved strip) — fixed inline during VALID-02.
- Final-show recap Share card showed all-time GizzDex totals instead of the night's show — resolved by the new per-show share card in 10-02.

**Issues Deferred:** none blocking. Non-blocking future UI ideas (directional-flow particles, bottom-sheet animation, full-date format) remain tracked as v2 todos.

**Technical Debt Incurred:** none material. Optional layer-2 tab-rename (internal code identifiers) remains deferred by design — display labels were rebranded (LiveGizz/GizzVerse/GizzDex), routes/storage keys deliberately unchanged.
